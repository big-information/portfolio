• David Marcus UX Lead at RedMane Technology

I'm a UX designer, researcher, and front-end developer who's been at it for 5 years now and loving every minute. Currently I'm the UX Lead at RedMane Technology in Chicago, where I lead our UX projects and design initiatives. On a typical day this involves both enterprise product development and consulting projects, as well as taking advantage of the occasional opportunity to spread UX knowledge throughout our company and serving as a UX subject matter expert.

I love owning UX from start to finish. I can't tell you how much I enjoy the user research, ideation, rapid prototyping, and software development process, especially when it involves working with diverse teams on ambitious projects that push the limits to what seemed impossible - until we did it!
