Just who is this David guy?

That's me! Hi I'm David, nice to meet you!

I'm a UX designer, researcher, and front-end developer who's been at it for 5 years now and loving every minute. Currently I'm the UX Lead at RedMane Technology in Chicago, where I lead our UX projects and design initiatives. On a typical day this involves both enterprise product development and consulting projects, as well as taking advantage of the occasional opportunity to spread UX knowledge and capability throughout our company.

As you can probably tell from my projects, I love owning UX from start to finish. I can't tell you how much I enjoy the user research, ideation, rapid prototyping, and software development process, especially when it involves working with diverse teams on ambitious projects that push the limits to what seemed impossible - until we did it!

Again, great to meet you. And don't be a stranger!
